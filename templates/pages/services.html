AI Menu Import

Upload a photo or PDF of your existing menu and our AI extracts dishes, descriptions, and prices automatically. Save hours of manual data entry and get your digital menu live in minutes. Perfect for restaurants migrating from paper or legacy systems.

Customizable Themes

Choose from a range of professional menu themes that match your brand. Adjust colors, fonts, and layout so your QR menu looks consistent with your restaurant’s identity. No design skills required—switch themes anytime from your dashboard.

Dedicated Support

Get help when you need it. Our support team is available to assist with setup, menu updates, and technical questions. Higher-tier plans include priority support and a dedicated success manager so you can focus on running your venue.

Menu Creation

Build and manage your menu with an intuitive editor. Add categories, items, descriptions, prices, and images. Drag-and-drop reordering, bulk edit, and duplicate items across locations. Your menu stays organized and easy for guests to browse on any device.

Multi-Service Management

Run dine-in, takeaway, and delivery from one place. Set different availability and pricing per service type, manage table and delivery zones, and keep orders flowing without switching between multiple tools.

Multilingual Support

Serve international guests in their language. Add multiple languages to your menu and let customers switch with one tap. Ideal for tourist areas, airports, and diverse neighborhoods. Translations are easy to manage from your panel.

Online Ordering

Customers browse your menu, add items to the cart, and checkout directly from their phone. Orders land in your dashboard and can be sent to the kitchen or POS. Reduce wait times and capture more orders with a smooth, mobile-first ordering flow.

PDF Export

Download your menu as a PDF for printing, sharing, or backup. Use the same layout and content as your digital menu so printed table cards and flyers stay in sync with your online version. One-click export from the dashboard.

Real-Time Updates

Change prices, mark items out of stock, or add daily specials—updates go live immediately for anyone scanning your QR code. No app update or cache delay. Keep your menu accurate and avoid disappointed guests.

Secure Payment Processing

Smart Order Management

See all orders in one queue, filter by status or time, and update preparation state. Notifications keep your team in sync. Optional integrations send orders to your POS or kitchen display so nothing gets lost between front and back of house.
